Hurricane Harvey

The first of the year's major tropical storms hit the gulf coast of Texas on August 25, 2017. Harvey made landfall as a Category 4 Hurricane with peak winds at 132 mph ENE of Port Aransas.s

NOAA's GOES-East satellite captured this visible image of Hurricane Harvey in the western Gulf of Mexico on Aug. 26 at 6:45 p.m. EDT. | Image: NASA/NOAA GOES Project

In addition to the devastating wind damage and storm surge in cities and towns along the coast, Harvey stalled over the area bringing historic amounts of rain to the nation's fourth most populous metropolitan area.

Costs

After an intense hurricane season that left Houston under water and Puerto Rico without power, experts expect it to be one of the most expensive hurricane seasons on record. Only 5 years since 1980 have had costs over $50 Billion, with 2005 being the most expensive to date at $212.9 billion following Hurricane Rita, Hurricane Wilma and the catastrophic Hurricane Katrina.

* 2017 values are based on estimates. The final values have not yet been determined and could exceed the estimated $200 Billion.
